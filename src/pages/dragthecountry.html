How to Play Democracy Game

  • 1
    Drag countries from the left panel to the correct government type on the right.
  • 2
    Government types: Democracy (free elections), Authoritarian (limited freedoms), Monarchy (royal rule), Totalitarian (total control).
  • 3
    Click "Check Answers" to see which countries you placed correctly. Green borders = correct, red borders = incorrect.
  • 4
    You have 10 attempts to get all countries correct. The timer tracks your progress.
  • 5
    Move countries back to the left panel to change your answers before checking.
