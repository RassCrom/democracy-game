🎯 How to Play

Welcome to Geography Facts!


🔍 Objective: Guess the mystery country based on the facts revealed!


📋 Game Rules:

• You'll receive up to 6 clues about a country

• Facts appear one by one - each wrong guess reveals a new fact

• The 6th and final clue shows the country's shape

• You have 60 seconds to make your guess


🎯 Scoring:

• Guess correctly on 1st fact: 100 points

• Guess correctly on 2nd fact: 80 points

• And so on... (minimum 10 points)


💡 Tip: Facts include population, capital, language, political system, and more!
